About me

I was a senior research scientist at the Thomas J. Watson Research Center, an IBM principal investigator at the MIT-IBM Watson AI Lab, and an IBM PI of the RPI-IBM AI Research Collaboration program. Also, I served as senior personnel at the NSF AI Institute for Future Edge Networks and Distributed Intelligence (AI-EDGE)

My research interests broadly lie in:

  • Foundations of machine learning models

  • Design of optimization algorithms and corresponding computational complexity analysis

  • Natural language processing, hierarchical learning, reinforcement learning, and decentralized learning
